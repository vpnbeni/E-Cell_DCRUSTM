Who we are?

E-cell DCRUST,Murthal is an institute body run by the students of DCRUST (Murthal) Haryana devoted to acting as a symbbiotic link between the entrepreneurs of E-Cell and the existing startup ecosystem as well as acting as a hub where all the startup can meet, collaborate and innovate!

What we do?

Through our vision of 'Learn, Build and Scale' we implement various initiatives and events in DCRUST,Murthal to faster the entrepreneurial minds and create a culture of enthralling startup bound for success!
